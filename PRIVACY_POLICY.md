# Privacy Policy for PhotoParty

**Last updated: January 2024**

## Overview

PhotoParty ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Photos
- PhotoParty accesses your device's photo library **only** when you actively select photos to share during gameplay
- We use Apple's PHPickerViewController which provides privacy-friendly access without requiring full library permissions
- Photos are **never** uploaded to external servers
- Photos are shared directly between players on your local network during gameplay
- Photos are held in memory temporarily and deleted when the game ends

### Local Network
- PhotoParty uses your local network to connect with other players
- We use Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework for secure peer-to-peer communication
- Network data stays on your local network and is never transmitted to external servers
- Player names you enter are shared with other players in your game session

### Analytics & Crash Data
- PhotoParty does not collect any analytics data
- We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services
- We do not collect crash reports

## Information We Don't Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** store your photos on any server
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** access your contacts
- We do **not** collect device identifiers
- We do **not** use advertising or ad tracking

## Data Storage

- All game data is stored locally on your device
- No user data is transmitted to or stored on external servers
- Game state is only shared between devices on your local network during active gameplay

## Third-Party Services

PhotoParty does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

PhotoParty is suitable for users ages 12+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Photos you share during gameplay are:
- Selected by you voluntarily
- Shared only with players in your current game session
- Deleted from memory when the game ends
